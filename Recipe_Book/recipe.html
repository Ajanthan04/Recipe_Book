Classic Avocado Toast

15 min
Breakfast
Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • Pinch of red pepper flakes
  • Sea salt and black pepper
  • Extra virgin olive oil (optional)
  • 1 Cherry tomato, quartered

Methods

  1. Toast the bread: Place the sourdough slices in a toaster or on a pan and toast until golden and crispy.
  2. Mash the avocado: Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a small bowl. Add the lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Mash with a fork until it reaches your desired consistency.
  3. Assemble: Spread the mashed avocado evenly on the toasted sourdough slices.
  4. Garnish: Top with quartered cherry tomatoes, a pinch of red pepper flakes, and a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil if desired.
  5. Serve immediately: Enjoy your delicious and healthy avocado toast!
